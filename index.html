Mining

Your objective is to reach as deep as you can before the timer runs out

Clearing a level gives you 5 gold

Going to the next level gives you 60 seconds

Contains 1 gold Moves to the next level
Enters the Shop

Press space bar or hold shift to switch to flag

Press esc to pause

Rules are similar* to minesweeper

The number represents worms in the surrounding blocks

Worms cause you to engage in combat


Combat

Combat consist of performing actions using items

Performing an action makes you tired for
1s per item weight

Click on left side to attack
Deals damage to enemy

Click on right side to defend
Reduces damage recieved for a short time
